# vosFoyer Organization Structure

## Overview

vosFoyer adalah 360 creative agency yang beroperasi dalam model Think → Build → Amplify. Struktur organisasi mencerminkan tiga fungsi utama: Commercial (client acquisition & management), Operation (service delivery), dan FAT & HRGA (support & infrastructure).

## Divisi Commercial

**Lead:** VP Commercial

### Busdev Department
- Busdev

### Account Department
- Account Manager
- Account Executive

## Divisi Operation

**Lead:** Operation Director

### Traffic
- Traffic:  1 orang, cross-department — bertanggung jawab atas project flow dan koordinasi antar department di Divisi Operation dan sebagai jembatan komunikasi ke Divisi Commercial.

### Strategy, Data & Planner Department
**Lead:** VP Strategy Data
- Strategist
- Data Analyst
- Planner

### Creative Department
**Lead:** Creative Director
- Art Director
- Designer
- Copywriter

### Production Department
**Lead:** Creative Director *(concurrent)*
- Producer
- Video Creator

### Media Buying Department
*(Lapor langsung ke Operation Director)*
- KOL Specialist
- Ads Specialist

### Social Media Department
**Lead:** Head of Social Media *(saat ini concurrent: Operation Director)*
- Social Media Specialist

## Inter-Department Relationships

| From                                | To                                  | Trigger                               | Type                 |
| ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Busdev Department                   | Strategy, Data & Planner Department | Client brief masuk / onboarding       | Handoff              |
| Strategy, Data & Planner Department | Creative Department                 | Strategy & content direction approved | Briefing             |
| Creative Department                 | Production Department               | Creative concept approved             | Production handoff   |
| Production Department               | Social Media Department             | Konten selesai diproduksi             | Distribution handoff |
| Social Media Department             | Strategy, Data & Planner Department | Monthly performance data              | Reporting loop       |
| Account Department                  | Media Buying Department             | Campaign budget & objective confirmed | Briefing             |
| Media Buying Department             | Account Department                  | Campaign report selesai               | Reporting handoff    |
| Divisi Commercial                   | Divisi FAT & HRGA                   | Invoice / PO dari klien               | Finance handoff      |
